How to Make an Origami Swan

  1. Start with your paper white side up, the colour will be the main body colour.
  2. Fold the paper in half diagonally and unfold.
  3. Fold the left and right edges into the middle.
  4. Flip the model over.
  1. Fold the left and right edges to the middle.
  2. Now fold the bottom corner up to the top.
  3. Fold down a section for the swan's head.
  1. Fold the top half backwards and behind as shown.
  2. Now while holding the bottom left section, pull up the head and neck.
  3. Decide on a position for the swan's neck and crease.
  4. You should now have a pretty origami swan that can stand up.
  5. If you ended up with a bit of a mess, don't worry, remember it takes practice to learn a new origami model, just make sure your folds are as precise as possible and it will turn out well in the end!
Voila!
